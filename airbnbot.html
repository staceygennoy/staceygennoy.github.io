Airbnbot

Airbnbot is a project exploring travel research. Recently I traveled to Japan and I used this trip as a reference point. Planning a trip has many facets and it is through using an A.I. chatbot offering automated suggestions that I aim to improve the travel planning process.

View my solution

Approach:

  • Social Human Computer Interaction model
  • Provide suggestions
  • Know the user's history, background and profile

Considerations:

  • How do people decide they want to go to Japan? The culture is very unique.
  • How do people decide where they should stay in Tokyo?
  • When is the best time to go?
  • How do people travel as a temporary locals?
